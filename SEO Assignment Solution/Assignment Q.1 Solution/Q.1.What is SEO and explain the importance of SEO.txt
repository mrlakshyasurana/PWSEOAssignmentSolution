Q.1.What is SEO and explain the importance of SEO ?

Ans:

SEO in Simple Terms:

SEO stands for search engine optimization. In simple terms, it means the process of improving your site to increase its visibility when people search for products or services related to your business in Google, Bing, and other search engines

Importance of SEO:

SEO, or search engine optimization, is a digital marketing strategy that helps your site rank at the top of organic search results for relevant keywords1. SEO is useful because it:
Makes certain changes to your website design and content that make your site more attractive to a search engine.
Helps you understand what users are searching for online so that you can create high-quality content to satisfy their intent.
Helps you create a website that search engines can find, index, and understand its content.
Increases your online visibility and brings qualified leads to your website.
